About

Dedicated to Advancing Cancer Care

Dr. Javed Khan is a Consultant Medical Oncologist at the Royal Midlands Cancer Centre and an Honorary Professor of Oncology at the University of Birmingham. He trained at King's College London Medical School and completed specialist registrar training at The Christie NHS Foundation Trust in Manchester.

His clinical practice spans solid tumours and haematological malignancies, with a subspecialty interest in lung cancer, lymphoma, and early-phase immunotherapy trials. He holds a PhD in tumour microenvironment biology and leads the Centre's Precision Oncology Programme.

Dr. Khan is committed to a patient-centred approach, ensuring every individual receives a clear, honest explanation of their diagnosis and access to the most current evidence-based treatments ‚Äî including clinical trial options where appropriate.
